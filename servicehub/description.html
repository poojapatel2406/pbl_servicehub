Plumbing Service

Professional plumbing solutions at your doorstep

Service Description

Our expert plumbers provide reliable repair and installation services for all your plumbing needs including leaking taps, blocked drains, pipe fittings, and bathroom/kitchen installations.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Customer must ensure access to the work area.
  • Service charges are exclusive of material costs.
  • Any additional work will be discussed before proceeding.
  • Warranty applies only to the work done by our staff.

Process Demo

Step 1: Book the service online and select a suitable time slot.
Step 2: A verified plumber is assigned based on your area and availability.
Step 3: The plumber visits your location and inspects the issue.
Step 4: Necessary repairs or installations are carried out.
Step 5: Payment is made after successful service completion.
